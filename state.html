My Statement

I am constantly learning about who I am and what I like to do with each project I work on. I am interested in the natural world. I like to critique and create parodies of societal norms in my art. I am learning to push my boundaries by incorporating things that are uncomfortable and unexplored in my art.

Videos that people see online or in movies always seem so easy and people don't really think about the process behind the scenes. I wanted to approach this by showing my audience a video where you can see everything go wrong in my “Unconventional” video. The “Growth” project aligned with my interests thinking about subjects in the progression of time. I have learned that I like working on projects that include changes based on the passage of time. In regards to the “Honeymart” website, I approached my strong dislike for ads in an interesting way. My abhorrence towards ads pushed me to create something that was full of them. Oddly enough, I became appreciative of the effort that goes into creating ads but I still don't like the approach they take.
